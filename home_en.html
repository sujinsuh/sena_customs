who we are

SENA Customs Consulting provides an EU import/export and customs related advisory service to Asian business, who operates an EU business or is willing to launch a business in EU.

We also provide a customs advisory service to EU business, who opearates an Asian business or is willing to launch a business in Asia. The CEO holds a Korean, Sigapore customs broker license as well as 3 years experience in Japan’s biggest accounting/customs consulting company.

We are specialised in Korea, Japan, and South East Asia including Singpaore from Asia side, and cover most of European countries from Europe side.

If you have any difficulty dealing with Asian Customs office, or would like to know about how their customs procedure works, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
